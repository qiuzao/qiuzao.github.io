A FEW THINGS ABOUT ME

I have worked in marketing, public relations and social work before I found my passion for UI/UX design. Now I’m induldging myself in a growing interest in mobile design and front-end coding.

Having worked/studied in Canada, China and UK, I always welcome the variety of cultures, ideas and styles.

Keeping myself in the learning mode makes me feel happy and secure.

I love designing from home with my cat, but I also enjoy the camaraderie of a team. In my spare time I love table-top gaming with friends, more design, and the combination of good food + a good movie.

Feel free to drop me a line, even just to say hi. Maybe we'll be co-workers, co-gamers, or good buddies for life.
