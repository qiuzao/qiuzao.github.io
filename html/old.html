Good Old Days

The old version of Qiuzao.net was in a more playful style. It was a simple one-page design, with a large header image. I designed a fox symbol and overlapped it on the letter "Q" to give it a feeling of leisureness.

The old site shows the works in a carousal window. Viewers may switch between works by clicking the hexagons below, though it does not allow space for reflective writings/case studies (which was the major reason why I decided to do a 2.0).

Becoming a Jedi warrior is, sadly, one of my dreams that did not come true. But well, at least I can do a self-portrait in Obi-Wan style for the "about" section...with two sabers! - There are more light saber vector tutorials out there than one can imagine, like this one from Tuts.
